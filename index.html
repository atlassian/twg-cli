Teamwork Graph CLI

TWG CLI is a command line interface to the Atlassian Teamwork Graph and Cloud services. The Teamwork Graph (TWG) is Atlassian's enterprise knowledge & context graph that continuously maps people, content, activities, and relationships across many work tools (Jira, Confluence, Google Drive, Slack, GitHub, Salesforce, etc.) in a compliant, permission aware fashion.

Install TWG CLI and your agent — Claude Code, Codex, Gemini, Cursor, and others — can query and act across all your Atlassian products and the Teamwork Graph on your behalf. Describe what you want in plain language; the agent handles the rest.

How it works

Install TWG CLI, authenticate, and set up agent skills. After that, just describe what you want:

  • "Summarize the work I did this week"
  • "What are my open Jira issues?"
  • "Show the current sprint for my team"
  • "Who do I collaborate with most?"

No commands to memorize. Your agent picks the right actions and runs them.

Products covered

  • Teamwork Graph — A context graph that connects everything your agents need
  • Jira — Work items, boards, sprints, dashboards, filters, transitions, links
  • Confluence — Pages, blogs, spaces, databases, whiteboards, search
  • Bitbucket — Repos, PRs, branches, commits, pipelines, deployments
  • Jira Service Management — Service desk, incidents, request types, queues, knowledge base
  • Assets — CMDB objects, schemas, and queries
  • Loom — Videos, spaces, and comments
  • Trello — Boards, lists, cards, and workspaces
  • Rovo — AI-powered search across your Atlassian site
  • Goals — Atlas goals with status updates and health tracking
  • Projects — Atlas projects with lifecycle management
  • Focus Areas — Strategic planning with hierarchy trees
  • Teams — Team discovery, membership, and org structure
  • Customer Service Management — External-facing customer support
  • Jira Align — Portfolio strategy and enterprise planning
  • Talent — Workforce planning and skills tracking
